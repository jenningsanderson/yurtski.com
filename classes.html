Avalanche and Backcountry Skiing Courses at YurtSki!

Yurt-based classes are all-inclusive, we provide lodging, meals, and all course-materials, see our suggested course packing list for what you should bring.

We offer classes at both our Big Belt Hut and YurtSki locations, see montanabackcountryyurts.com/classes for the complete list. Below are the courses happening at YurtSki only.

This winter we're offering the following 3 types of courses at YurtSki: AIARE Avalanche Level 1, Level 2, and Backcountry Skills (Women's Only)

Need to rent gear for a class?

Visit MT Ski Tech in Missoula ($229 for skis, boots, beacon, shovel, and probe when you tell them you're taking a class with YurtSki!) Or head to Last Best Ski & Paddle for Black Crow AT skis or Weston Splitboards with Spark bindings (beacons, shovels, probes available, too).

AIARE Level 1 Avalanche Course

3-day, 2-night all-inclusive Level 1 avalanche course taught by AIARE certified instructors at both our Big Belt Hut and YurtSki locations. $600.

Expand Description >

Yurt-based courses mean we can spend more time in the field! Montana Backcountry Yurts will provide meals, lodging, and course-materials for the students. Course cost is $600. See the sample itinerary for a daily breakdown.

This is an official AIARE Level 1 avalanche course for all types of winter backcountry travelers.

Familiarity and experience with the operation of your backcountry gear (A/T bindings / splitboards / skins) are required for this course since we will be traveling into and around the yurts.

Backcountry Skills

This is the perfect course for someone who has limited or no experience in backcountry skiing and wants to gain confidence in order to join in the backcountry fun!

Expand Description >

We will go over the basics of backcountry travel including skinning techniques, essential gear needs and use, and basic avalanche awareness training.

The women's backcountry-skills clinic is an introduction to backcountry ski/snowboarding exclusively for women.
