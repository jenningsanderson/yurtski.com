Avalanche and Backcountry Skiing Courses at YurtSki!

2020 - 2021 Winter Courses Update: Non-yurt based AIARE Classes

Due to COVID-19, we do not feel that it is safe to host our yurt-based avalanche courses with a bunch of new ski-friends confined to the close-quarters of a yurt. That being said, we do think that providing avalanche classes this winter is extremely important for our backcountry community. Therefore, we have restructured our courses to be as safe as possible during this pandemic while also still providing the same excellent AIARE Level 1 and 2 curriculum and instruction our students love.

Hopefully next season we can get back to those Yurt based courses and scrumptious catered dinners. Additionally, we are fully booked this season on arranging private avalanche courses, but email us about discussing options for the winter 2021/2022 season if you have a crew of 4+ that is interested in a private avalanche course! info@montanabackcountryyurts.com



AIARE Level 1 Avalanche Course

3-day course: 1 online and 2 field days in the backcountry outside of Lincoln, MT where we can socially distance outside and still get hands-on with the snowpack. $450.

Expand Description >

Classes will be town based (no food or lodging provided) and operate as follows:

  • Students will complete a 1 day AIARE classroom session online via 4(ish) hours of student self-paced learning coupled with 4(ish) hours of video conference classroom instruction.
  • Classes will vary depending on the instructor. Students should plan to be on a video conference call for at least Noon-5pm the Friday of their course.
  • Students will then complete 2 field days (Saturday and Sunday ~8am - 5pm) in the backcountry outside of Lincoln, MT where we can be outside, social distance, and wear masks (something us winter recreationalists are great at already!)

This is an official AIARE Level 1 avalanche course for all types of winter backcountry travelers.

Familiarity and experience with the operation of your backcountry gear (A/T bindings / splitboards / skins) are required for this course since we will be traveling into the backcountry for the class.

AIARE Level 2

4-day course: 1 online and 3 field days in the backcountry outside of Lincoln, MT where we can socially distance outside and still get hands-on with the snowpack. $550.
