Getting to the Trailhead

Click Here for directions (Google Maps)

The YurtSki parking lot is located a few hundred yards past Rich's Montana Guest Ranch on Cottonwood Lakes Rd. You will see a large white enclosed snowmobile trailer on the right and a parking spaces to your left. Please carpool with other members of your party as the shared parking area is small.

Getting to the Yurts

Download this map

The yurts are located 5 miles up the Morrell Mountain Lookout Rd., the turnoff for which is 5 miles from the trailhead via Cottonwood Lakes Rd. Be cautious of logging operations during the week within the first 3 miles of Cottonwood Lakes Rd. The above map will get you from the trailhead to the yurts.

Skiing at the Yurts

YurtSki trail map
Install this map on your phone!

Do this before you leave your house, once installed, you don't need cell service for the maps to work

  1. View this page on your phone at yurtski.com/maps
  2. Download Avenza Maps for iOS or Android.
  3. Open the following maps on your phone:
    • 1. Trailhead to Yurts Map
    • 2. YurtSki Ski Runs Map
  4. Click on the share icon and choose Copy to Avenza Maps for each map.

These maps are now available in the Avenza Maps app and when you are in the area, a blue dot will show your exact location without the need for cellular coverage.
