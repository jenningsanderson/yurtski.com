### Essentials
- Beacon
- Shovel
- Probe
- Skis/ Splitboard & Skins (or snowboard and snowshoes)
- Poles
- GPS/Compass and Morrell Mountain Quad Map
- First Aid Kid 
- Sleeping Bag
- Headlamp
- Hat/Helmet
- Goggles
- Warm, non-cotton insulating layers
- Waterproof outer layers

- Extra batteries for beacons and headlamps.

### Recommended
- Yurt Slippers
- Fleece / Down coat
