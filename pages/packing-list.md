### What To Bring?
- Beacon
- Shovel
- Probe
- Skis/ Splitboard & Skins (or snowboard and snowshoes)
- Poles
- GPS/Compass and Morrell Mountain Quad Map
- First Aid Kid 
- Sleeping Bag
- Headlamp
- Hat/Helmet
- Goggles
- Warm, non-cotton insulating layers
- Waterproof outer layers
- Extra batteries for beacons and headlamps.
- Toilet Paper
- Paper Towels / Rags

### Recommended Additional Items
- Yurt Slippers
- Fleece / Down coat
- Camera
- Radios

### Cell Phones
There is spotty cell reception at the Yurt and along the ridge. Do not rely on having reception for navigation or communications.

#### How much beer?
Cans are known to jostle and break in a fully loaded gear sled over 11 miles. Large groups planning on bringing ~ 80 cans of beer should consider purchasing a pony keg.
