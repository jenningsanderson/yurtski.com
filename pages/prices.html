Snowmobile Gear Haul + Tow

We have large gear hauling toboggans to take the weight off your back (and allow you to bring more beer)! The snowmobile route to the yurt is 11 miles. We will tow your gear all the way to the yurt.

The ski route to the yurt is only 8 miles. Gear haul includes a tow rope for members of your group for all but 1.5 miles of the 8 mile ski. These 1.5 miles include a 1,500ft climb. If there is a lot of new snow, our snowmobile driver will bring his skis and can break in the skin track for you if requested. Click here for the map.

To accomdate guests to both yurts, we offer two fixed times for gear hauls: 8:30am and 11:30am. These are first-come, first-serve time slots purchased online with your reservation at the base prices below. If you would like to book any other time that works better for your group, perhaps after work or one of the fixed times already reserved by another party, our gear haul drivers are happy to accomodate for a $50 surcharge.

Each toboggan is approximately 3' by 8' and can carry up to 400 lbs of gear. A group of 6 for 3 nights can usually fit all of their gear in one toboggan.

We will try to fit your gear into one toboggan; however, if an additional gear haul is required, we charge an additional $200 per toboggan.

Yurt Rental Policy

  • We collect 100% of your trip cost up front at the time of your booking.

  • Each yurt is exclusive, we do not offer open beds to other parties.

Cancellation

You can cancel your reservation for a 50% refund up to 30 days prior to your trip start date. After that we cannot offer a refund. To cancel, please email or call us at (406) 848-4824
