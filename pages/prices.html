Snowmobile Gear Haul + Tow

We have large gear hauling toboggans to take the weight off your back (and allow you to bring more beer)! The snowmobile route to the yurt is 11 miles. We will tow your gear all the way to the yurt.

The ski route to the yurt is only 8 miles. Gear haul includes a tow rope for members of your group for all but 1.5 miles of the 8 mile ski. These 1.5 miles include a 1,500ft climb. If there is a lot of new snow, our snowmobile driver will bring his skis and can break in the skin track for you if requested. We will send you maps and more informtion with confirmation of your reservation.

To accommodate guests to both yurts, we offer two fixed times for gear hauls: 8:30am and 11:30am. These are first-come, first-serve time slots purchased online with your reservation at the base prices below. If you would like to book any time that works better for your group, perhaps after work or one of the fixed times already reserved by another party, our gear haul drivers are happy to accomodate for a $50 surcharge.

Yurt Rental Policy

YurtSki requires a 50% nonrefundable deposit at the time of booking.

There is a "Pay Now" link in your invoice that lets you pay the remaining balance up to 30 days before your trip. We will charge any outstanding balance to your credit card 30 days prior to your trip. If booking less than 30 days in advance, we require you to pay in full.

Using the "Pay Now" link also allows you to login or create an account (simple and free) with yurtski.checkfront.com, where you can modify or cancel your bookings up to 30 days prior to your start date. Perhaps you decided you want a gear haul? Maybe an extra toboggan? You can do it all online!

Cancellation

You can cancel your trip up to 30 days prior to your start date online, by email: info@yurtski.com, or by phone: (406) 848-4824. Your deposit will not be refunded, but you will not be charged for the remaining balance.

Trips cancelled less than 1 month prior to the start date are still required to be paid in full and are nonrefundable. We will charge the remaining balance to the card used at booking.
