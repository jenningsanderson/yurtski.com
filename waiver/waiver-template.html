Montana Backcountry Yurts & YurtSki Waiver 2018

In consideration for my being permitted to participate in the activities of backcountry skiing/riding, Cross-country skiing, snowmobiling, and/or snowshoeing I agree to the following Waiver and Release:

I acknowledge that winter backcountry travel has inherent risks, hazards, and dangers for anyone that cannot be eliminated, particularly in a wilderness environment.

I UNDERSTAND THAT THESE RISKS, HAZARDS, AND DANGERS INCLUDE WITHOUT LIMITATION:

  1. Traveling and route finding in Avalanche terrain;
  2. Skiing and Hiking in rugged country;
  3. Injuries from skiing/boarding, snowshoeing and its equipment;
  4. Encounters with wildlife, animals, and insects;
  5. Temperature extremes;
  6. Inclement weather conditions and unavailability of immediate medical attention in the wilderness in case of injury.
[@initial-box-1]

I understand the risks, hazards, and dangers of backcountry skiing, snowboarding, cross country skiing and/or snowshoeing and have had the opportunity to discuss with MONTANA BACKCOUNTRY YURTS LLC, DBA.YURTSKI. I understand that these activities may require good physical conditioning and a degree of skill and knowledge. I believe I have that good physical conditioning and the degree of skill and knowledge necessary for me to engage in these activities safely. I understand that I have responsibilities. My participation in this activity is purely voluntary. No one is forcing me to participate and I elect to participate in spite of the risks.

[@initial-box-2]

BACKCOUNTRY SKIING IS A HAZARDOUS SPORT. All Skiers who participate in services provided by YurtSki understand and agree that he or she is competent in backcountry ski skills, and safety, knows and voluntarily assumes any and all risks of skiing (touring and downhill) in the backcountry including: Travel in avalanche hazard terrain. Avalanche terrain assessment and safe travel techniques. This includes skiing to and from the hut(s) and anywhere in the vicinity of or around or about the hut(s) during the time the Skier is renting the hut(s).

I acknowledge the risks of backcountry skiing and understand that I am required to carry the minimum required avalanche equipment: digital avalanche beacon, shovel, probe. I am educated and practiced in the use this equipment.

I AM VOLUNTARILY USING THE SERVICES OF MONTANA BACKCOUNTRY YURTS LLC, DBA.YURTSKI WITH FULL KNOWLEDGE OF THE INHERENT RISKS, HAZARDS, AND DANGERS INVOLVED AND HEREBY ASSUME AND ACCEPT ANY AND ALL RISKS OF INJURY, PARALYSIS, OR DEATH.

Lastly, I, for heirs, my successors, executors, subrogees, and myself hereby KNOWINGLY AND INTENTIONALLY WAIVE AND RELEASE, INDEMNIFY AND HOLD HARMLESS MONTANA BACKCOUNTRY YURTS LLC, DBA.YURTSKI, their directors, officers, agents, employees and/or volunteers from and against any and all claims, actions, causes of action, liabilities, suits, expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) which are related to, arise out of, or are in any way connected with my participation in this activity including, but not limited to: NEGLIGENCE of any kind or nature, whether foreseen or unforeseen, arising directly or indirectly out of any damage, loss, injury, paralysis, or death to me or my property as a result of my engaging in these activities or the use of these services, animals or equipment., whether such damage, loss, injury, paralysis, or death results from negligence of MONTANA BACKCOUNTRY YURTS LLC. DBA YURTSKI or it's guides or from some other cause.

I, for my heirs, successors, executors, subrogees, and myself hereby further agree not to sue as a result of any injury, paralysis, or death suffered in connection with my use and participation in the activities of backcountry travel including, but not limited to skiing/boarding, snowshoeing, and/or snowmobiling.
